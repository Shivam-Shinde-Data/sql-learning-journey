# Day 32 – RANK() Interview Questions

## 1. What is RANK()?
Assigns rank to rows, allowing ties.

---

## 2. What happens when values are equal?
Same rank is assigned.

---

## 3. Does RANK skip numbers?
Yes, after ties.

---

## 4. Difference between RANK and ROW_NUMBER?
RANK allows ties, ROW_NUMBER does not.

---

## 5. Difference between RANK and DENSE_RANK?
RANK skips numbers, DENSE_RANK does not.

---

## 6. Use case of RANK?
Leaderboards, competition ranking.

---

## 7. Can RANK be used with PARTITION BY?
Yes.

---

## 8. Does RANK require ORDER BY?
Yes.

---

## 9. Can RANK return multiple top rows?
Yes, if ties exist.

---

## 10. Real-world example?
Top scoring students with same marks share rank.
