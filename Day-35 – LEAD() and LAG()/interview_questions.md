# Day 35 – LEAD() and LAG() Interview Questions

## 1. What is LAG()?
Returns previous row value.

---

## 2. What is LEAD()?
Returns next row value.

---

## 3. Difference between LEAD and LAG?
LEAD → forward, LAG → backward.

---

## 4. What is offset?
Number of rows to skip.

---

## 5. Default value in LEAD/LAG?
Used when no row exists.

---

## 6. Does it require ORDER BY?
Yes.

---

## 7. Can we use PARTITION BY?
Yes.

---

## 8. Real-world use?
Trend analysis, previous comparison.

---

## 9. Can we replace self join using LAG/LEAD?
Yes.

---

## 10. Why are they important?
Used heavily in analytics and time-series queries.
